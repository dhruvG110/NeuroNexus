FAQ

What can Brave Leo do?

Leo can summarize webpages, generate content, translate, analyze text, and more—all within your browser.

What platforms is Leo available on? Can I try Leo on mobile?

Yes, Leo is available on both desktop and mobile versions of the Brave browser.

What LLMs does Leo have access to?

Leo can access multiple language models, depending on availability and user preferences.

Can I use my own models in Leo?

Currently, Leo uses Brave-hosted models. Personal model integration isn't available yet.

What data does the Brave browser send to the servers that power Leo?

Brave minimizes data sharing. Requests are proxied and stripped of user-identifying information.

Does Brave Leo have a daily limit on the number of prompts I can send?

Yes, there may be fair-use limits depending on model load or your subscription plan.

Do I need to sign up for a Brave account to use Brave Leo?

No sign-up is required to use the basic version of Leo. For premium features, a Brave account may be needed.

Will you use my conversations for model training?

No, Leo's Brave-hosted models do not retain or use your data for training.

What are Brave-hosted models?

These are AI models hosted by Brave that do not share data with third parties or retain user interactions.
